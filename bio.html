Hello, I'm Cat! (and I'm an actual person)

I'm a writer and cartoonist attending the University of Florida. I spend my days finishing up my undergraduate degree in journalism, improvising with UF's Theatre Strike Force, and drawing, writing and copyediting for the not-school newspaper, The Independent Florida Alligator.

My work is greatly influenced by my childhood.

I grew up with my feet in the dirt, my head in a book, and my arms holding a cat.

And ever since, I have continued to be inspired by nature, stories, and my feline friends.
